## The brief

Bon House is an Atlanta venue building an audience, and it needed a web presence
that matched the room: confident, quiet, and quick. The work was the front of house
online, a site that sets a tone before anyone walks in.

## The build

A TypeScript and Next.js site on a small design system, so events, listings, and new
sections can be added without the layout drifting. The priorities were a fast first
paint, clean typography, and a structure the venue can keep current without a
developer in the loop for every change.
